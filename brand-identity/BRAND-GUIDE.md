# Gerimis Brand Identity Guide

## Brand Name
**Gerimis** /guh-REE-miss/ — Indonesian for "drizzle", a light gentle rain.

## Tagline
"Embrace the Drizzle"

## Brand Story
Gerimis creates colorful, artfully designed rain ponchos from recycled materials. Born in a small studio in Canggu, Bali, the brand celebrates rain as something beautiful — not something to hide from. Every design is inspired by Indonesian landscapes: jungle canopies, coral reefs, temple offerings, rice terraces, and tropical sunsets.

## Target Audience
- Hipster / creative types in Bali, Jakarta, and Southeast Asia
- Age 22-38, fashion-conscious, sustainability-minded
- Digital nomads, surfers, creatives, café culture
- Values: self-expression, sustainability, color, joy

## Typography

### Display Font
**Playfair Display** — Serif, used for headings and brand name
- Weights: Light (300), Regular (400)
- Usage: Hero headings, section titles, product names

### Body Font
**DM Sans** — Sans-serif, used for body text and UI
- Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- Usage: Navigation, descriptions, buttons, labels

### Text Styles
- **Micro labels**: 10px, uppercase, tracking 0.3em, accent color
- **Navigation**: 12px, uppercase, tracking 0.15em
- **Body**: 14px, gray-600, relaxed line height
- **Headings**: Playfair Display, light weight

### Logo Usage Rules
- Minimum clear space: 1x height of the icon on all sides
- Minimum size: 24px height for digital, 10mm for print
- On product: Small embroidered logo on left chest or woven label on back yoke
- Never stretch, rotate, or alter colors

## Design Principles

### 1. Joyful Color
Every touchpoint should feel colorful and alive. No beige, no boring.

### 2. Editorial Warmth
Clean layouts with generous whitespace, but warm — cream backgrounds, not stark white.

### 3. Island Casual
Premium but relaxed. Think Canggu café, not corporate office.

### 4. Sustainability Without Preaching
Show don't tell. Materials facts, not lectures.

## Photography Style

### Product Photography
- Clean flat-lay or hanging shots
- Soft natural light, slight window shadow
- Cream/light grey backgrounds
- Show fabric texture and pattern detail
- Small, subtle GERIMIS branding visible (embroidery or label)

### Lifestyle Photography
- Real models in Bali/Jakarta settings
- Rice terraces, beach walks, scooter rides, café culture
- Candid, natural poses — not staged fashion
- Diverse models, mixed ages
- Always in the rain or overcast weather preferred
- Show the poncho in action — movement, joy, real life

## Spacing & Layout
- Border radius: 0-2px (sharp, editorial)
- Card padding: generous (p-5 to p-8)
- Section spacing: 16-24 units between sections
- Max content width: 7xl (1280px)
- Grid: 4 columns desktop, 2 mobile for products

## UI Components
- Buttons: Filled accent color or outlined black, no border radius
- Cards: Subtle rounded corners, sand background
- Accordions: Minimal, + icon rotates to x
- Cart: Slide-out from right
- Newsletter: Modal popup, 10% off incentive
